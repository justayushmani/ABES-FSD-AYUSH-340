Top Destinations

Paris , France

Experience the romance and charm of Paris, with its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Don't forget to indulge in some delicious French pastries!

List of Activities

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Stroll along the Seine River
  • Enjoy a boat cruise
  • Discover Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur

Tokyo, Japan

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Tokyo, where tradition meets modernity. From ancient temples to cutting-edge technology, Tokyo offers a unique blend of experiences.

List of Activities

  • Visit the historic Senso-ji Temple
  • Explore the bustling Shibuya Crossing
  • Shop in Harajuku
  • Experience a traditional tea ceremony
  • Enjoy panoramic views from Tokyo Skytree

New York ,USA

Discover the energy and diversity of New York City, known as "The Big Apple." From iconic landmarks to world-class museums, there's something for everyone in this bustling metropolis.

List of Activities

  • Visit the Statue of Liberty
  • Explore Central Park
  • See a Broadway show
  • Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge
