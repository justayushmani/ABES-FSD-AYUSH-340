Tech Club is a student organization dedicated to fostering interest and knowledge in technology among college students. Our club provides a platform for students to explore various aspects of technology, including software development, hardware engineering, cybersecurity, and emerging tech trends. We organize workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and collaborative projects to enhance members' skills and encourage innovation. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced tech enthusiast, Tech Club offers opportunities to learn, network, and contribute to exciting tech initiatives within the college community
